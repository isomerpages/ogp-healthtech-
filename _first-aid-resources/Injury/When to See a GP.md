<p>See a GP if you experience the following:</p>
<p></p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Minor cuts or scrapes that can be cleaned and dressed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minor sprains or strains.</p>
</li>
</ol>
