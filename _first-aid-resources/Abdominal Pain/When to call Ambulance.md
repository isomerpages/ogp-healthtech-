<h3>Call an ambulance if you experienced the following symptoms: </h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Severe pain with inability to walk.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fainting due to the abdominal pain</p>
</li>
</ol>
