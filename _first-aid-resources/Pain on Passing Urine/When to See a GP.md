<h3>See a GP if you experience the following:</h3>
<p></p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Mild pain or burning sensation while urinating, with a frequent need to
go.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A feeling of incomplete bladder emptying.</p>
</li>
</ol>
